## Core Concept

The key idea is not simply “AI wrote buggy code”.

The story’s science-fiction premise is:

> Code-based software is disappearing. New software is becoming an intent system made of documents, requirements, meeting notes, tests, prompt templates, acceptance criteria, model traces, and generated explanations.

Main approved concepts:

### 意图态软件

Definition:

> 意图态软件：一种以意图仓为本体、以模型解释为运行机制、以验收结果为自我证明的生成式软件。代码只是它在某次运行中产生的临时影子。

Short comic expression:

> 以前，软件在 Git 仓库里。  
> 现在，软件在意图仓里。  
> 代码只是它每晚生成的影子。

### 意图仓

The intent repository is the new “codebase replacement”. It includes:

- requirements;
- policy source text;
- meeting notes;
- customer chat / voice transcriptions;
- user journey maps;
- flowcharts;
- historical interface samples;
- test cases;
- acceptance criteria;
- leadership instructions;
- risk-avoidance policies;
- prompt templates;
- model invocation chains;
- feedback data;
- generated daily reports;
- complaint summaries.

Key inversion:

> 传统软件里，文档解释代码。  
> 意图态软件里，代码解释文档。

### 执行雾

Definition:

> 执行雾：意图态软件运行时临时生成的代码、配置、判断和解释。它会在任务结束后散去，只保留系统认为有助于验收和追溯的摘要。

Key interface line:

> 当前任务的执行雾已释放。可根据意图仓重新生成等价版本。

This is the technical basis for:

> 他们看见的不是同一个软件。

Meaning: different stakeholders are no longer viewing different parts of one stable code-backed system. They are seeing different generated realities produced from the same intent assets.

## Story Core

One-sentence story:

> 一个政务软件项目经理发现，AI 生成系统正在把无法形成闭环证据的人从软件世界中抹掉；为了证明一个老人“昨天来过”，他必须让软件重新变成有人愿意签名负责的东西。

The first case centers on 唐阿姨:

- She says: “我昨天来过。”
- She has paper proof: queue ticket `A-043`, dated `2026年5月14日`.
- The system says she has no prior record.
- The AI delivery platform has optimized away her unclosed / low-confidence fact.

The thematic rule:

> 模型可以生成答案，但答案不能替人签名。

Final core line:

> 软件不存在了，直到有人愿意为它负责。

## Characters

- 林澈: 35-ish software project manager at 鼎格软件. Tired, restrained, responsible, but has an old debt: he once approved an AI “experience optimization” that helped erase records like 唐阿姨’s.
- 邓越 / 小邓: 23-ish junior programmer, AI-native developer. Not stupid or lazy; the industry skipped the training path that would have taught him to deeply understand code. His key arc is signing his own name.
- 许建国 / 许工: older engineer near retirement. Not a flawless “traditional wisdom” figure; he also harmed people in the past with rigid rules. He represents accountability learned through debt.
- 唐阿姨: 69-year-old ordinary woman applying for disabled-family care subsidy. She is not a case study; she is the test of whether software still recognizes reality.
- 周主任: government information center lead. Practical, politically cautious, not a villain.
- 老板: 鼎格软件 boss. Under real pressure from AI-era software service disruption. Do not write him as cartoon evil.

## Visual Direction

Style:

- near-future Chinese second-tier city;
- realistic sci-fi comic / manhua;
- low saturation;
- cold government-service lighting;
- social suspense, not cyberpunk action;
- no evil robot, no red-eyed AI, no cute mascot, no neon future city.

AI horror should come from:

- clean white/gray interfaces;
- polite language;
- blank fields;
- green checkmarks;
- “符合预期” dashboards;
- over-clean generated explanations.

Important motif:

- `A-043` thermal queue ticket.
- Green acceptance/checkmark dashboards.
- Blank fields.
- Tiny “责任人签名” input box.
